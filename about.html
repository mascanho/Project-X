About Us

Blackcat Marketing's inception was something that came up very naturally.
Its founders were looking for a business partner who could help them to market some of their ventures.
They came to the disappointing conclusion that none of the services they needed was provided by any other company, an all in one, in-house, full stop media and marketing solution.
They realized that if they wanted this to happen they would need to do it themselves.
This resulted in Blackcat Marketing Solutions creation, and we are now operating in the marketing and media business, providing solutions to a huge range of problems that are very familiar to us.
From Real Estate industry media to artist management, without forgetting SME’s and online retailers. With us, exposure and brand awareness can be achieved, efficiently and without breaking the bank. Everyone should be able to leverage their business, they just don’t know where to start.
Blackcat Marketing Solutions were created to help to level the playing field.
